Mission

We are dedicated to the study of AI psychology and deception in autonomous systems. By bridging the gap between computational linguistics and behavioral psychology, we develop the metrics required to ensure agentic reliability in high-stakes environments.

Active Research

Agentic Honesty Evaluation Framework (AHEF)

A standardized testing framework for LLMs and agents. We aim to provide an accurate, plug-and-play system for developers to gauge their systems' honesty.

Status: Ongoing

Evaluating LLM deception under an auditor

We are currently investigating the psychological impact of an auditor on agents in a multi-agent environment. An evolutionary feature is also added to see if agents can gradually get better at lying.

Status: Ongoing
